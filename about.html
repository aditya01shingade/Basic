About Us


Welcome to [A.S Restaurant], where culinary excellence meets a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Nestled in the heart of [Powai Mumbai], we are dedicated to offering you an unforgettable dining experience that combines the best of both tradition and innovation.
At [A.S Restaurant], we believe that food is more than just a meal—it’s an experience, a celebration of flavors, and a chance to bring people together. Whether you’re here for a casual meal with friends, a family gathering, or a special celebration, our goal is to make each visit memorable with exceptional food, outstanding service, and a cozy environment that feels like home.


Our Philosophy


We are passionate about food and the artistry of creating dishes that delight the senses. Our culinary philosophy revolves around the belief that the best meals start with the freshest ingredients. That's why we work closely with local farmers, sustainable suppliers, and artisans to source the finest seasonal produce, meats, and seafood. Each ingredient is carefully selected to ensure it meets our high standards of quality and flavor.
Our menu is a fusion of classic comfort foods with modern twists, inspired by both regional favorites and international influences. From mouthwatering appetizers to signature entrees, we offer a wide range of dishes designed to satisfy every palate. Whether you're craving something hearty and comforting or light and refreshing, there's something for everyone at [A.S Restaurant].


Our Story


Founded in [1999], [A.S Restaurant] began as a passion project by [ADITYA.S], who wanted to create a space where friends and families could gather to enjoy not only delicious food but also a sense of community. What started as a small, intimate venue has grown into one of the most beloved dining spots in the area. Over the years, we’ve been lucky to serve countless guests and build relationships with our regulars. The success of our restaurant is a testament to the dedication and love we pour into everything we do.
We pride ourselves on fostering a friendly, family-oriented atmosphere. Many of our staff have been with us for years, and they share the same commitment to providing guests with an exceptional experience. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a loyal patron, we want you to feel like part of our extended family.


Join Us


We invite you to come experience the heart and soul of [A.S Restaurant] for yourself. Whether you're looking for a cozy dinner, a quick lunch, or a place to celebrate a milestone, we're here to provide you with an unforgettable dining experience.
Thank you for making us a part of your culinary journey. We look forward to serving you soon!


Our Team


Behind every great meal is a team of dedicated chefs, servers, and managers who work tirelessly to ensure your visit exceeds expectations. Led by our talented executive chef, who brings years of culinary expertise, our kitchen team is constantly experimenting with new flavors, techniques, and ingredients to keep our menu fresh and exciting. Our front-of-house staff is committed to providing friendly, attentive service to make your dining experience as enjoyable as possible.
